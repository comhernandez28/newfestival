WHAT KIND OF SECURITY AND MEDICAL STAFF WILL YOU HAVE AT THE FESTIVAL?

We have extensive security and medical staff onsite. If, at any time during the festival you need any assistance, seek out the medical tent, or look for a nearby security officer or festival staff member. Security is of paramount importance, as it is our priority to provide a safe and enjoyable weekend for all of our guests.

HOW MUCH IS THE ENTRANCE FEE?

HOW MUCH IS THE ENTRANCE FEE?

TICKETS- Entrance to the festival is $20 pre-sale and $25 at the door. (Each Day) Children under age 12 are FREE but must be accompanied by an adult. Senior Citizens over 65 are free with valid ID as well as Veterans and Active Military with ID. No children under age 12 allowed on festival site after 5:30pm. No refunds after admission is paid shall be honored. Guests must request optional admission discount upon entry.

WHERE DOES THE PARADE START?

WHERE DOES THE PARADE START?

Parade steps-off is at 10:30am on Ocean and Lindero Ave. Parade proceeds west along Ocean Blvd. to conclude at Alamitos Blvd. near the Festival grounds. Parade Volunteers are always welcome, and are asked to report to the Volunteer Check-In at Ocean Blvd. and Coronado Ave.

WHAT CAN I BRING TO THE FESTIVAL?

WHAT CAN I BRING TO THE FESTIVAL?

For everyone’s safety, these conditions will be stricly enforced by Festival Security and Staff: NO Broadcast or Industrial Cameras are allowed without going through the Media Application process. NO distribution of pamphlets, fliers, handbills, etc., will be allowed on festival grounds. NO roving solicitation of any kind. NO outside beverage containers allowed into the festival grounds. NO PETS. Neither EXOTIC nor DOMESTIC animals will be allowed on the festival grounds. Service animals (with authentication documents) will be the only exception. NO folding chairs, bikes, skates or rollerblades.

I'M ALMOST 21 , CAN I BUY BEER?

I'M ALMOST 21 , CAN I BUY BEER?

NO, we strictly enforce a ZERO-TOLERANCE policy of underage consumption of alcohol. Any guest under the age of 21 found consuming alcohol will immediately be evicted from the festival.

=
?
IS THERE RE-ENTRY THIS YEAR?

IS THERE RE-ENTRY THIS YEAR?

Due to new restrictions placed on Long Beach Lesbian and Gay Pride by the ABC (Alcoholic Beverage Control), City of Long Beach and Long Beach Police Department in order for patrons to continue to be able to move freely about at the festival while enjoying an alcoholic beverage, Long Beach Pride is not allowing re-entry. Given our event is private we are still under ABC jurisdiction and of the options offered by the ABC and LBPD the no-entry decision will have the least impact on our event. While this restriction is new, we are confident that it will not impact your Pride experience. We thank you for your understanding and support. We look forward to creating a meaningful and memorable 2016 Pride together.
